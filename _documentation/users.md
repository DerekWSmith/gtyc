## Notes

- **Titles** (Commodore, Secretary, etc.) are honorific/display only — they do not control access.
- **Admin** and **Event Officer** flags control actual permissions.
- All committee members can log in and see their title badge in the navbar.
- Only Admin and Event Officer users see the "Manage Roster" and "Manage Events" links.
